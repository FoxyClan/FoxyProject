DNA & Rarity

Each NFT has unique DNA that represents traits. For example, DNA 071402070810 indicates the following trait numbers:

Head Covering : 07
Eyes : 14
Mouth : 02
Clothes : 07
Fur : 08
Background : 10

The rarity scale for each trait ranges from 00 (rarest) to 15 (least rare). This means that the rarest combinations are also the most valuable.
Each DNA is unique and can only be specific to one NFT. In the same way, no NFT can have the same DNA with simply a change of background or transcendence, so characters without their background are also unique.

Level & FoxyPoints

Each NFT Foxy has a Level and FoxyPoints. A Foxy gains a level when merged with another (Foxy the maximum level is 3).
FoxyPoints can be earned during a merge (+100 FP for a first merger and +250 FP for the second), but also during future missions or games. A Foxy can automatically earn points every day based on their level. No one yet knows what these mysterious FP are for, but an old legend says that they are very valuable and allow you to obtain large rewards later on...

Merge

One of the most exciting features of our collection is the ability to merge two NFTs you own.
This process allows you to create an advanced NFT with:

  • ➤ Level +1
  • ➤ The FoxyPoints of both Foxies + FP merge bonus
  • ➤ The best traits of each Foxy + the possibility of having enhanced traits
  • ➤ Possibility of having a Transcendence trait
  • ➤ Increased FP earned daily.

Merge rules :

- Two Foxies can only be merged if one of them is level 1.
- A level 3 Foxy can no longer be merged.
- If the merged DNA already exists, then the DNA is enhanced to remain unique.
- If the DNA cannot be enhanced, then the two NFTs cannot be merged.
- An undiscovered Foxy cannot be merged.
- When two Foxies are merged, both NFTs are burned and a third one with a new token ID is created.
- Burned NFTs are not rebuilt, so the total supply will permanently decrease.
